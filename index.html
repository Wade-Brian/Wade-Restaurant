Wade Restaurant - Karibu Kenya!

Appetizers

  • Samosa

    Crispy pastry filled with spiced minced meat or vegetable.

  • Bhajia

    Potatp slices coated in spiced chickpea flour and deep-fried.

  • Mahamri ya Nazi

    Swahili coconut doughnuts, best paired with chai.

Main Courses

  • Nyama choma

    Grilled goat or beef served with kachumbari & Ugali.

  • Pilau

    Fragrant spiced rice cooked with beef or chicken.

  • Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

    Maize meal served with sauteed collard greens.

Desserts

  • Mandazi

    Soft, slightly sweet fried dough often enjoyed with tea.

  • Kaimati

    Sweet dumplings coated with sugar cyrup.

  • Mango Delight

    Fresh Mango slices served with a drizzle of honey and lime.

  • Mandazi ya Mkate wa Sinia

    Pillowy Swahili doughnuts dusted with cinnamon sugar.

Drinks & Beverages

  • Chai

    Kenyan-styled spiced tea brewed with milk and sugar

  • Mursik

    Traditional fermented milk popular among Kalenjin community.

  • Fresh Fruit Juice

    Rereshing juice made from seasonal fruits like mango, pineapple or passion.

  • Soda

    Popular soft drinks including Coca-Cola, Fanta and Sprite.

  • Dawa

    Kenya's famous cocktail: vodka,honey,lime and crushed ice.

  • Tusker Lager

    Kenya's iconic beer, brewed since 1922.
